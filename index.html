Henry & Lucky's Love Story

Our Journey Together

It all began with simple Snapchats and getting to know each other. Little did we know, this was the start of something truly special.

One boring Sunday in 2023, a spontaneous idea led us to Nairobi National Park! We walked, talked, and took countless pictures, making beautiful memories.

Valentine's Day 2024 was filled with love and laughter. We spent the day at the Nairobi Orphanage, enjoying every moment with our hearts full.

More unforgettable moments at the Nairobi Orphanage this year! Every visit brings us closer and fills our days with joy.

Countless walks and talks, just enjoying each other's company. These simple moments mean the world to us.

Our Future Together

Our journey is just beginning. We dream of building a beautiful life together, starting in **Nakuru**, where we can create our first home. We're ready to work hard, hand-in-hand, to achieve our dreams.

We envision a future with both a **traditional wedding** to honor our heritage and a **beautiful church wedding** to celebrate our love in faith. We can't wait for the day we can call each other husband and wife, grow our family, and get rich together!
